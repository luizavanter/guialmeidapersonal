<div class="service-body">
<h2>Healthy Weight Loss</h2>
<p class="service-subtitle">Lose Weight Sustainably</p>
<p>Program focused on weight loss through proven strategies that preserve muscle mass and accelerate metabolism.</p>
<div class="service-columns">
<div>
<h4>What's included</h4>
<ul>
<li>Resistance training to preserve and build muscle</li>
<li>Strategic cardio protocols to maximize fat burning</li>
<li>Basic nutrition guidance (macronutrients and timing)</li>
<li>Weekly monitoring of measurements and bioimpedance</li>
<li>Frequent adjustments to maintain constant progress</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<h4>Ideal for</h4>
<ul>
<li>Those who need to lose 5kg or more</li>
<li>People who have tried diets without success</li>
<li>Those who want to lose weight without losing muscle mass</li>
<li>Anyone seeking habit change, not just temporary results</li>
</ul>
</div>
</div>
<div class="service-result">
<span class="result-label">Expected Results</span>
<p>Loss of 0.5-1kg of fat per week, muscle mass preservation, improved lab results, increased energy and self-esteem.</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>

<p class="service-subtitle">Build the Body You've Always Wanted</p>
<p>Specialized program in muscular hypertrophy with advanced periodization and load progression techniques.</p>

<li>Recovery protocols and injury prevention</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div>
<h4>Ideal for</h4>
<ul>
<li>Those who want to gain lean muscle mass</li>

<li>Experienced practitioners looking to break through plateaus</li>
<li>Men and women over 40</li>
</ul>
